About Baku

Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is a city with a rich history and culture. Located on the southern shore of the Absheron Peninsula, Baku is a city of contrasts, where ancient architecture meets modern design.

Places to Visit

  • Old Town

    Explore the narrow streets and historic buildings of Baku's Old Town.

  • Maiden Tower

    Visit the iconic Maiden Tower, a symbol of Baku's rich history.

  • Heydar Aliyev Center

    Discover the stunning architecture and cultural exhibitions of the Heydar Aliyev Center.
